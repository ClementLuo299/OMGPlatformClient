## Point Schemes for Potential Card Games

For Crazy Eights:
The first winner gets 2 points, second winner gets 1 point

For Cribbage:
Winner gets 3, loser gets 1
Winning by 30 points gets 1 point, 60 points gets 2
Loser gets additional point they score within 10 of the winner

For (German) Whist:
Playing gives 1 point
Winning gets 1 point
Quick is 3 rounds
Long is 10 rounds
